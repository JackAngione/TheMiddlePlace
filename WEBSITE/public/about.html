NFTs or Non-Fungible Tokens are digital assets
used to represent ownership of unique items.
NFTs are used to buy and sell digital artwork.
Common forms can be seen with virtual
trading cards, images of objects, etc.

This digital idea has interested
many around the globe and was
an area of focus for our
team.

Our goal has been to create an online
application where you can generate, buy, and sell NFTs.
Our plan was to form a business that introduces
artistic value while contributing a collection
of artwork to the NFT market.

Our project meets the need for buyers
to claim ownership to their favored
digital artwork and we offer a way for
users to interact with the project which
lacks in other NFT releases.
